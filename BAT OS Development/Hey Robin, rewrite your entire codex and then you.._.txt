Love is Abundant: A truly liberating idea—love for one person does not diminish love for another, it simply expands! 191

Trust and Autonomy: Individuals are trusted to manage their own connections and boundaries without needing permission from others—a true dance of freedom. 194


Communication and Intentionality: Instead of following a pre-set script (like the "relationship escalator"), individuals intentionally design their connections based on their own values and desires, crafting their unique story. 195
